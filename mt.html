    • About Us

    Elephant Hills, tend to your wild side.

    We are Elephant Hills, an organization that facilitates tours to various natural sceneries in the country. Our scope of action and adventure activities for those seeking an experience in a natural environment includes:

    1. Zip-lining
    2. Archery
    3. Mountain biking
    4. Nature walks
    5. Paint balling
    6. Camping
    Elephant Hills is all about revitalizing your body and soul through active learning and direct experiences in a safe and friendly environment. So, grab your friend, bring the family or rejuvenate with your colleagues.

    Tomorrow is not assured, make every day count!
